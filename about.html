About

Insurance Made Simple, Honest, and Personal

At Wealth and Health Insurance Agency, we help everyday people get access to the coverage they need — without the confusion. Whether you`re an individual looking for an affordable health plan, a parent protecting your family, or a small business owner trying to offer benefits, we`re here to make the process clear, easy, and stress-free.

We`re based in Florida and proudly serve clients across the state — in both English and Spanish.

What We Do

  • Affordable Care Act (ACA) Health Insurance - Marketplace plans based on income and household size.
  • Private Health Plans - For those who don`t qualify for subsidies or want different options.
  • Life Insurance - Term, whole, and final expense policies to protect your family.
  • Annuities - Retirement income solutions with safety and guarantees.
  • Small Business Coverage - Group health plans for your growing team.

You`ll never be pushed into something you don`t need. We`re independent — which means we work for you, not the insurance companies.

Our Approach

  • Clear conversations
  • Respect and transparency
  • Fast, responsive support
  • Bilingual service (Se Habla Español)

Every recommendation we give is based on your needs, not a sales quota.
